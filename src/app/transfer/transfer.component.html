Arrival, how to reach us?


 Split Airport - Split Ferry Port

From Split airport you can take bus or taxi. If you choose to call our transfer organizer Emir (see contact us page), you will get price reduction. He can also organize bigger or more cars depending on how many you are. Taxitrip takes around 20-30 minutes depending on traffic. Price example: 300 kuna or 40 euro for a car up to four people.

  Split Ferry Port -Vis Ferry Port

From Split you will take a ferry to Vis. There are 3 different ferrys.

  • Jadrolinija, ticket is 54 HRK per person and it takes 2hr and 15min. Departures 3 times a day. If you have your own car with you then this is the choice.

  • Kapetan Luka, ticket is 55 HRK per person and it takes 1hr and 15min to Vis. Departures 1-2 a day. This is a smaller and faster boat. You can bring your bicycle on it.

    Vis Ferry Port - VisitAylinVis apartments

From Vis Ferry Port you can take taxi or use our pick-up service. It is a short 15 minutes trip with nice views. Narrow roads on hight can be scary for some people. Price example: ordinary taxi cost around 160 kuna for a car up to four people. Our pick-up service cost only 100 kn. We offer return trip on your departure day for same price of 100 kuna undependent on which ferry you have booked.
